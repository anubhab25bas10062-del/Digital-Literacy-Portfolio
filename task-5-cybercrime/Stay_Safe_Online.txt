Stay Safe Online - Checklist

i. Use Strong, Unique Passwords: Create passwords with a mix of letters, numbers and symbols. Avoid using the same passwords across apps

ii. Enable two factor Authentication: Turn on Two factor Authentication for email, social media, and banking apps to add an extra security layer.

iii. Do not click Suspicious inks: Avoid clicking on unknown links for emails, SMS or Whatsapp - even if they look urgent and official.

iv. Verify before Sharing personal Information: Never share OTPs, Passwords, or personal details with anyone, even if they claim to be from a bank or service provider.

v. UPI Safety: Before sending money, double check the UPI ID or QR Code to avoid sending money to the wrong person.

vi. Never approve unknown payment requests: Scammers may send "Collect Request" on apps like Google Pay or Phone Pe - do not approve unless you trust the sender.

vii. Avoid using Public Wi-Fi for transactions: Public networks can be unsafe. Use mobile data or secure networks for banking or  payments.

viii. Cyber Fraud: Report online at the National Cyber Crime Portal or Call the Cyber Crime Helpline:- 1930
