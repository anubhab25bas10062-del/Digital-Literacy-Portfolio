Responsible Social Media Use

Do's:
i. Maintain a profeessional and respectful tone in your posts and comments.
ii. Think before posting - ensure your content reflects your values and goals.
iii. Protect your privacy by adjusting account settings and liniting personal information.
iv. Verify information before sharing to avoid spreading misinformation.
v. Use social media for learning, networking and skil developement.

Dont's:
i. Do not share sensitive personal information (address, phone number, passwords)
ii. Avoid posting offensive, harmful or inappropriate content
iii. Do not engage in online arguements, trolling or cyberbullying
iv. Avoid oversharing details about your personal or academic life
v. Do not let social media distract you from studies and responsibilities
